---
title: Picking a hero image
tags: images, meta
---

Figures aren't the only thing you can nominate. An uploaded image opts in the
same way — give it a `"preview"` title and it becomes the cover, no matter where
it sits in the entry.

This first shot appears earlier, so the old logic would have used it:

![Rolling hills at golden hour](https://picsum.photos/seed/hero-a/800/500)

But this is the one I actually want on the card, so it carries the tag:

![A neon city street after rain](https://picsum.photos/seed/hero-b/800/500 "preview")

The match is exact: only a title of exactly `preview` counts. A real caption
that happens to contain the word — like `"a preview of next week"` — is left
alone and never mistaken for the tag.
