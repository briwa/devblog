title: Field notes, no pictures
tags: writing
---

Not every entry has a figure or an image, and that's fine. With nothing to show,
the card falls back to a plain text excerpt drawn from the opening paragraph —
exactly this sentence, trimmed to fit.

Some weeks the work is all reading and small refactors: no screenshots worth
keeping, nothing to animate. The entry still earns its place in the journal, it
just wears words instead of a cover.
